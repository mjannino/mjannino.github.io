Welcome to Blackjack!

This is a project for Software Engineering 1 by Michael Jannino.

The rules are simple:

  • The goal is to reach 21 by either "hitting" or "staying"
  • The game starts out with two cards, with one of the dealer's face-down
  • Face cards count as 10
  • Aces can count as 1 or 11
  • You cannot exceed 21; in the event of the dealer and you exceeding 21, house wins
  • For each hand won, you gain 10 points, and for each hand lost, you lose 10 points; in a tie, you gain 5; pocket aces for a dealer is a bust and immediate blackjack is a win (house rules)
